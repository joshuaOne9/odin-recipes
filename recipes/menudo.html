Menudo

Menudo "Rojo"

A traditional Mexican soup made with tripe and hominy. What makes it "rojo" is the addition of red chili peppers, which gives the soup its distinctive red color and a rich, spicy flavor. For extra flavor, add some cilantor and use limes or lemons to squeeze on top of the soup. When choosing the tripe, look for pieces that are clean and have a good amount of fate, as this will add flavor and tenderness to the soup, preferably honeycomb tripe, which has a unique texture that absorbs the flavors of the broth well. Menudo is often enjoyed as a hearty and comforting dish, especially during colder months of the year!
