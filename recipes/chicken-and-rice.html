Chicken and Rice

This Chicken and Rice is a simple, budget-friendly comfort meal where boneless, skinless chicken thighs are coated in a flavorful blend of paprika, oregano, thyme, garlic powder, and onion powder, then seared until golden brown in a deep skillet. After removing the chicken, diced onion is sautéed in the same pan to pick up all those delicious browned bits, followed by toasting the long-grain white rice for a minute or two before pouring in vegetable broth. The chicken goes back on top of the rice, the lid goes on, and everything simmers together on low heat for 20 minutes — no peeking allowed — followed by a 5-minute rest. The result is perfectly cooked, fluffy rice infused with all the savory seasonings and chicken juices, topped with tender, juicy chicken thighs and a sprinkle of fresh parsley.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1.25 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs (4–5 thighs)
  • 2 Tbsp cooking oil, divided
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice (uncooked)
  • 1 3/4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 Tbsp chopped parsley (optional garnish)
